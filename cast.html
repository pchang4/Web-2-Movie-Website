Director: Julius Avery

Julius Avery is an Australian screenwriter and film director. After growing up in Pemberton, Western Australia, Avery attended The Victorian College of the Arts in Melbourne. He has written and directed several award winning short films including Cannes jury prize winning short film Jerrycan in 2008.
